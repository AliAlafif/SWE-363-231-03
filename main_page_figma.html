About Al-Hilal

Al-Hilal SFC is a professional football club based in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, which competes in the Saudi Pro League, the country’s premier football league. Founded on October 16, 1957, Al-Hilal SFC is one of the most successful clubs in the Middle East, having won several domestic and international trophies over the years.
